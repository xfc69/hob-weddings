December the 3rd, 2024, Leila and Sami celebrated their love under the golden glow of Dar Zarrouk, Sidi Bou Said. The crisp winter air was filled with laughter, music, and the scent of jasmine drifting from nearby gardens. Sami’s eyes sparkled as he watched Leila glide across the terrace, her dress catching the soft sunlight. Friends and family gathered around, toasting their union with joy and warmth. Every glance, every embrace, every fleeting moment was captured with care, creating a visual story of a day brimming with emotion, elegance, and timeless love.
