About
Hob Weddings
Hob Weddings was founded by Yassin Hannachi and Ala Sinen, two artists united by a passion for capturing love. Yassin, with seven years of experience in wedding cinematography, and Ala, a fine-art photography graduate from Carthage University of Arts, combine their expertise to craft imagery that is emotional, authentic, and timeless. Together, they turned their passion into a studio dedicated to preserving love stories with artistry and care.
